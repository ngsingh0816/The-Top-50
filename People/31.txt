Ludwig Van Beethoven
Beethoven wrote many symphonies and has many classics that are known to almost everybody world wide. The most notable of which was Fur Elise, which Beethoven wrote when he was completely deaf. He played piano so often that he memorized the sound each note made. Ludwig Van Beethoven was influential because he made unforgettable classics and he showed that even if one had a disability such as being deaf, one could still be important in society.
