William Shakespeare
Shakespeare, a playwright who lived in England during the 15th and 16th century, wrote many classic plays, sonnets, and poems. Although there is no proof that Shakespeare even existed, there is no doubt that person or group of people that wrote his plays were very influential. Plays like Romeo and Juliet played with the heart, while other plays like Othello and Macbeth played with the mind. His works have inspired people to make plays of their own and be creative. Therefore, William Shakespeare was influential because of all the mind blowing plays and poems he wrote.
