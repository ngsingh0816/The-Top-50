Walt Disney
The classical mastermind Walt Disney was the brain behind all of the Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck based stories. Throughout Disney's career, he created many cartoons that provided comical relief for the ordinary worker. After a while, Disney teamed up with Pixar to create films that people thought to be the best animated films that one could make. Walt Disney is influential because he made making cartoons a general and lucrative business.
