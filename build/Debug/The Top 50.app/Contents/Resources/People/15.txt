Joseph Stalin
Joseph Stalin was the leader of the Soviet Union during World War II. During his reign, Stalin killed many people and betrayed Hitler after forming an alliance with him. Stalin then allowed Russia to join forces with the United States but soon after, tensions rose which led to the Cold War. Stalin is influential because of how many people he impacted during World War II.
