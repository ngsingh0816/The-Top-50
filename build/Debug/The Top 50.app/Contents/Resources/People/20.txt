Tim Berners Lee
Tim Berners Lee is responsible for creating the World Wide Web. Before Lee, the all one could do on the internet was email. But after the World Wide Web was created, one could surf the web, get any information they desired, and much more. Tim Berners Lee is influential because he revolutionized the way we use computers and made it much more convenient to find information.
