James Watt
James Watt is founder of the steam engine, which allowed machines to have more power to do more energy consuming tasks. This helped in production and travel as both rely on machinery. As a result of creating the steam engine, the Industrial Revolution started. This is because the steam engine laid the path for new inventions to be created.
