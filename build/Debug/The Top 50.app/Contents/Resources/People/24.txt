Qin Shi Huang Di
Once a great emperor of China, Qin Shi Huang Di had a very advanced civilization. He united most of China, which was remarkable at the time because of its size, and was technologically ahead. He had crossbows the assembly line, and chrome hundred of years before other people ever thought of them. In addition to that, his workers were able to made a real size sculpture for every soldier in the Chinese army. He is influential because of the people he united and the inventions that were made as an outcome of unification.
