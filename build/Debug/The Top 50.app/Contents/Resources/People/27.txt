Michelangelo
Michelangelo was an influential painter who painted the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel. He influenced many people with his work because of how amazingly he created the precision and depth of the chapel in such a small space. Michelangelo was influential because he created the ceiling of one of the man-made wonders of the world.
