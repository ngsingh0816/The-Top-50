Nikola Tesla
Tesla was a mad scientist who has a imaginative mindset. He like to create things that were essentially possible. Or at least were thought to be at the time. He laid the foundation with his work on trying to develop wifi and radio. Nikola Tesla also invented the Tesla Coil, which is used to power many things such as x-rays. Today, Tesla is credited with creating the radio and has patents on many of the parts that make up the radio. Tesla is influential because he helped to create things that every person in the modern world uses every day.
