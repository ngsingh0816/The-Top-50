Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
Mozart was a incredibly talented musician who composed many symphonies at a young age. He revolutionized music and inspired people to create more music. In his lifetime, he composed over 600 different songs. Mozart's advancements in classical music will never be forgotten as 200 years later, there is not a single person who has not heard one of his symphonies. Mozart is influential because he created many works that were popular throughout time.
