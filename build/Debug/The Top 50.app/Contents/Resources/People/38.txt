Louis Pasteur
Pasteur revolutionized science especially medical science because of many reasons. First of all, he developed the first vaccines for rabies and anthrax, both of which can be bad diseases. Second of all, he proved the first germ theory where diseases were caused by bacteria in the blood. Before people thought that diseases were caused by "badness" in the blood. Third of all, Luis Pasteur prevented milk and wine from making people sick by using a process which he developed called pasteurization. Overall, Luis Pasteur is influential because he made several advances in medical science that would save many peoples lives.
