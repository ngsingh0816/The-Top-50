Attila the Hun
Attila the Hun was the nomadic tribe leader of the Huns who pillaged and took over many cities. He caused an attack on the Visigoths who were then forced to move to the west, where Rome use to lay. Because of the Visigoths coming in with no place to stay, there were forced to seek shelter in Rome, but the only way they could do that was if they conquered Rome. Eventually, Rome and many other empires or cities fell because Attila the Hun and that is why he is influential.
