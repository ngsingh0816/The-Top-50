Leonardo Da Vinci
Leonardo Da Vinci was a painter, scientist, and inventor who created soma e of the most influential things to ever exist. One of these influential works was called "The Mona Lisa", a picture of a woman that is said to contain a great amount of hidden messages. Also, Da Vinci had the blueprints for many inventions like the steam engine a long time before they were actually invented. Therefore, Leonardo Da Vinci is influential because he created many things that still inspired people today and gave basic outlines for some important inventions that were to come a later time than he was alive.
